# Featured Post Example

This is an example of a **featured post**! Featured posts appear at the top of the blog list with a special badge and styling.

## How to Create a Featured Post

Simply add `Featured: true` to your post's front matter:

```markdown
Tags: featured, example
Date: 2025-11-04
Featured: true

# Your Post Title

Your content here...
```

## Benefits of Featured Posts

- **Highlighted visibility** - Featured posts appear first in the list
- **Special badge** - Marked with a ‚≠ê Featured badge
- **Enhanced styling** - Distinct visual treatment
- **Perfect for announcements** - Great for important updates or popular content

Featured posts are sorted by date among themselves, then regular posts follow.
